Property Values and Neighborhood Characteristics in Santa Monica

The factors determining the housing value have been a popular research topic in both academic and business field. These factors can be generalized to three different aspects: Housing Characteristics, Demographic Factors and Geographical Variables. A housing property is more variable if it is newly built and located in decent neighborhood. In this project, the geographical section will be specified and the other elements will be considered as control variables for the housing price evaluation. After controlling demographic and property characteristics, I am particularly interested in the effect of accessibility to commercial services to the property values. The following map penal shows the neighborhood characteristics for median property value, median income, median square footage of the buildings , and the traveling distance to the commercial districts. The right line graph display the property values of commercial and residential buildings in each percentile.

The distribution of the residential properties is very different from the commercial one. The magnitude of the difference is enlarged for the properties in higher percentiles. The marginal effect on property value from building profitability is higher than the one from improvement in living qualities.
The distribution of property values have slightly changed over time in Los Angeles County. We do not observe significant difference in the shape of the distribution where it is normally distributed with wide spread of extreme high-valued properties. Moreover, the median value of the whole county grown from 270,000 to 310,000 between 2014 and 2018.
Santa Monica region in Los Angeles County is an appropriate case for the project for several reasons. Firstly, public regional railway system in Greater Los Angeles Area is limited, and automobile remains the most common way for transportation. Thus, the average traveling time of Uber could be a close approximation. Secondly, the heavy industry with pollution which negatively affects the housing price is negligible. On the contrary, the existence of startup tech companies in the region might be positively correlated to the housing price. By concluding the above information, properties are expected to be more valuable if they are closer to commercial districts after demographic and housing patterns are controlled. The main purpose of this project is to test this hypothesis and discover the distribution of the effects.
