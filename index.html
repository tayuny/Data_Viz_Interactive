Property Values and Neighborhood Characteristics in Santa Monica

The factors determining the housing value have been a popular research topic in both academic and business field. These factors can be generalized to three different aspects: Housing Characteristics, Demographic Factors and Geographical Variables. A housing property is more variable if it is newly built and located in decent neighborhood. In this project, the geographical section will be specified and the other elements will be considered as control variables for the housing price evaluation. After controlling demographic and property characteristics, I am particularly interested in the effect of accessibility to commercial services to the property values.
